Madrid was our last stop of the 2 week journey. It was here where I met 2 of my best friends, Ellie and Zhara. It was here where I learned that you don't need to conform into friend groups in order to meet cool people!

Zhara is from New Jersey, and Ellie is from Wisconsin, which also showed me that the best adventures you can have may come from people with totally different lives from yours :D

For lunch, we stopped at a place called "Caja Sorpresa" also called the Kawaii Cafe in Madrid. We all ordered some marble soda together, and I decided to try out the orange flavor while Ellie and Zhara both had blue raspberry flavored marble soda.
