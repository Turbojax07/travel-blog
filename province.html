And finally, we have the most ICONIC Barcelonian architecture... get ready!!

La Sagrada Familia is the LARGEST unfinished church in the world. The entire church building will be finished by the start of 2026 in celebration for Christmas.

This huge cathedral was created by Gaudi as a way to express his devotion towards Catholicism. The architecture depicts the birth of Christ (Christmas) on the very bottom, the crucifixion of Christ in the middle, and Christ's ascension to heaven at the soon built top.
